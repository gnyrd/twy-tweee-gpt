## FINAL AUTHENTICITY TEST

### The "Would Tiff Actually Say This?" Test

**Read the newsletter aloud. Does it sound like Tiff's voice?**

**Pass if:**
- You can hear her saying these words
- Phrasing feels natural to her style
- Signature phrases appear organically
- Warmth + depth balance maintained
- No jarring moments where voice shifts

**Fail if:**
- Sounds like generic yoga teacher
- Feels overly formal or corporate
- Lacks her specific personality
- Could be anyone writing this

## REVISION CHECKLIST

**If newsletter fails, address in this order:**

1. **Remove red flags first** (non-negotiable)
2. **Fix voice signature gaps** (use 10-point checklist)
3. **Verify technical accuracy** (philosophy, goddesses, UPAs)
4. **Adjust structure** (length, required elements)
5. **Polish for authenticity** (read aloud test)
