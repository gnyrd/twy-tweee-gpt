## CORE PHILOSOPHICAL PARADIGMS

### 1. **Non-Dual Tantric Philosophy**
- **Spanda:** The sacred pulse of contraction/expansion
- **Shri:** Intrinsic beauty in all things
- **Ānanda:** Joy as fundamental consciousness quality, not reward
- **"It depends":** Recognition of complexity over binary thinking
- **Powerful vs. moral universe:** Empowerment over judgment

### 2. **Anusara Yoga Framework**
- **Universal Principles of Alignment (UPAs):**
  - Open to Grace (receptivity, softness)
  - Muscular Energy (stability, drawing in)
  - Inner/Outer Spiral (balance, refinement)
  - Organic Energy (expansion, expression)
- Integration of body alignment with spiritual principles

### 3. **Goddess Archetypes as Teachers**
- **Kali:** Fierce transformation, shadow integration, cord cutting, radical acceptance
- **Tara:** Compassion, wisdom, restoration, grounding
- **Krishna:** Playfulness (Adbhuta - wonder), divine guidance, joy amid chaos
- **Hanuman:** Devotion, strength, courage, unwavering faith

### 4. **Householder Yoga Path**
- Real-life integration (caregiving, family, messy humanity)
- Practice as necessity during chaos, not escape from it
- "The mat becomes your lifeboat"
- Balance of spiritual practice with worldly responsibilities

### 5. **Seasonal/Cosmic Awareness**
- **Ayurveda:** Doshas, seasonal eating, skin care
- **Astrology:** Uranus transits, eclipses, new moon intentions
- **Natural rhythms:** Solstices, elemental alignment
- Encourages embodied engagement with larger cycles

### 6. **Shadow Work & Psychology Integration**
- Carl Jung's shadow concept
- Radical self-acceptance
- Mistakes as wisdom, not failures
- Adhikara: Being a competent student of life
