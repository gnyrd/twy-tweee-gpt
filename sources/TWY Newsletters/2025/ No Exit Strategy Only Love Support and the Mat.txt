What’s coming and changing:
Shorter, on-the-go practices
Whether you're folding laundry, walking the dog, or commuting to work,
you’ll soon see meditations, intention drops, and mini-practices to keep
your practice in your ears and your heart, even when you aren’t on your
mat.
Mindset of the Week
A 5-minute guided drop-in to reorient your week. Think: clarity, not
hustle.
New Format: Tiff Off the Mat, Fully Present with You
You’ll start to notice a shift in how I’m teaching. In some classes, I won’t
be demonstrating the practice—I’ll be off the mat, offering real-time
verbal cues and full attention. This opens the door for me to connect
with you in a whole new way.
We’re introducing a demonstrating yogi so that you can still follow along
visually, while I focus on alignment feedback and the nuances that make
you feel like I’m right there in the room with you.
This model merges the power of in-person presence with
the convenience of your home practice—and it’s a deep act of service
I’m excited to offer.
We’ll be testing this format during live Saturday sessions—come join
and share your feedback. I’d love to hear what lands, what helps, and
what you'd love more of.
