For the Yogis Who’ve Drifted
If your mat feels distant, it’s okay. Yoga is always here, waiting for you.
Start small.

Just Begin: Five minutes, one pose, a single breath—it counts. This
is my GO TO advice for myself and all yogis who need
encouragement to practice. Stop making it about the whole
practice…. FIVE MINUTES and then you can quit if you’re too busy
or feel disconnected.
Join us for even the centering part of a practice or the Savasana…
or the warm up. YOU deserve this!
This Season, Keep It Simple
Yoga isn’t about doing it all. It’s about showing up. A few mindful
moments on your mat ripple into everything else.
Roll out your mat. Take a breath. We’ll meet you there.
